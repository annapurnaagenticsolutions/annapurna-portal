Privacy Policy

WonderHub by Annapurna Agentic Solutions

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Summary

WonderHub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens in your browser. No accounts, no login, no tracking.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies (except those required for offline PWA functionality)
  • No data sent to any server
  • No third-party services or SDKs
  • No advertising or ad SDKs

How the App Works

WonderHub is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. It serves HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files directly to your browser. There is no backend server, no database, and no API. The app works entirely offline once loaded.

Local Storage

The app may use your browser's local storage and service worker cache to enable offline functionality. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

WonderHub is designed for learners of all ages. Since we collect no data, there is no risk to children's privacy. No parental consent is needed because no data is collected.

Links to Other Sites

WonderHub links to other Annapurna learning websites. Each has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page. Since we collect no data, changes will only be for clarity.
