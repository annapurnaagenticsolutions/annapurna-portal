Our Story

Honesty first. Open source by default. Built in India, for the world.

1

What We Build

AI governance tools, agent infrastructure, a typed DSL for agent workflows, education platforms, MSME applications, and client websites. Some products are free under MIT. A few premium products use a royalty model — code stays open, commercial use funds development.

Who We Serve

Developers, students, parents, educators, small business owners, and schools across India and globally. Our free products serve everyone. Our premium products serve businesses that need specialized, production-grade tools with commercial licensing.

Our Values

Principles that guide every product.

1

Open Source by Default

Most of what we build is MIT licensed — free for commercial and non-commercial use, no attribution required. We believe open source builds adoption and trust. Premium products use Apache 2.0 with a royalty rider, not restrictive closed-source licensing.

2

Honesty First

No fake statistics, no inflated claims, no marketing fluff. What we say exists, exists. What we say works, works. Transparency over hype.

3

Built in India, for the World

Offline-capable, multi-language, low-bandwidth, mobile-first. Designed for India's constraints — millions of small businesses, 22 official languages, uneven internet access. Then made available to everyone, everywhere.

Licensing Model

Community Service

Building for those who need it most.

1

Free Education for All

We build education platforms that work offline, in multiple languages, on low-end devices. No paywalls, no ads, no data collection. Built for students, parents, and educators who cannot afford paid platforms. Because education shouldn't depend on internet speed or device quality.

2

Tools for Small Businesses

India has millions of small businesses that can't afford SaaS subscriptions. We build free tools that work offline, in local languages, on basic phones. No sign-ups, no data harvesting, no hidden costs. Just tools that help.

3

Open Source as Public Infrastructure

We treat open source as public infrastructure — roads and bridges for the digital economy. Free to use, free to modify, free to build upon. The royalty model on premium products funds the free ones. Sustainable by design.

Technology

Our tech philosophy.

1

Static-First

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build steps, no frameworks where avoidable. Fast, portable, easy to host on GitHub Pages or any static host.

2

Offline-Capable

PWAs with service workers. Our products work without internet. Designed for India's uneven connectivity.

3

Minimal Dependencies

Zero or near-zero npm/pip dependencies. Simple, maintainable, long-lasting code. No framework churn.
