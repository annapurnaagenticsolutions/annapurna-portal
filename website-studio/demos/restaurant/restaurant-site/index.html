Our Story

Founded in 2010, Feliciano has become a culinary destination known for its warm hospitality and exquisite dishes. Our master chefs source only the finest, freshest ingredients from local farmers and markets to create a menu that celebrates traditional recipes with a modern twist.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is a masterpiece, crafted with passion and designed to provide you with an unforgettable dining experience in a truly elegant setting.
