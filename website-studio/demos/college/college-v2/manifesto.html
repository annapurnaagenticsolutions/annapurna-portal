The Radical
Manifesto

01. Art that conforms to existing market algorithms is dead upon conception. We design for the unmapped future.
02. Discipline is not rigid repetition. It is the obsessive pursuit of mastering tools only to shatter their perceived limitations.
03. Every student is an independent studio. Faculty are not lecturers; they are provocative collaborators and critics.
