What we build for clients

We build static websites, PWAs, education platforms, and AI tools. Most of our products are open source under MIT — free for everyone. For client projects, we offer one-time builds and retainer arrangements tailored to your needs, timeline, and budget.

  • Static websites — HTML/CSS, no frameworks, fast and portable
  • Progressive Web Apps — offline-capable, multi-language, mobile-first
  • AI tool integration — agent workflows, governance, evaluation
  • Education platforms — K-12, digital literacy, STEM, AI literacy
  • MSME applications — business tools for India's small businesses
  • Custom development — tell us what you need
Royalty-based licensing for premium products. A small set of our products use Apache 2.0 with a commercial royalty rider instead of restrictive closed-source licensing. This keeps the code open for non-commercial use while sustaining development through commercial adoption. If you're interested in licensing, integrating, or reselling any of our premium products, reach out and we'll discuss terms.
